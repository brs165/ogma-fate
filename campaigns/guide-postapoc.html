◻ The Long Road

The Beautiful Apocalypse

The world collapsed. Nature came back. The highways are forests. The cities are coral reefs of concrete and vine. Survivors scavenge what the old world left - lethal, tempting, occasionally still powered on. The Water Wars define local politics. The Reclamation Cult preaches that scavenging is sin. And somewhere, the Clean Zone draws people like a compass needle.

Tone & Atmosphere

Lyrical post-apocalypse. The world is dangerous but beautiful. Nature is not hostile - it is indifferent, and its indifference is gorgeous. The pre-war technology is miraculous and cursed. The question is not how to survive but what kind of world you build with what you salvage.

For Players

You are scavengers, healers, scouts, and builders. Every piece of the old world has a price. The settlements need what you find. The road gangs want what you carry. The Reclamation Cult says what you do is blasphemy.

Key Factions

Settlement councils (fragile democracies), road gangs (territorial raiders), the Reclamation Cult (ruins are sacred, nature is the correction), trade caravans, vault communities (pre-war survivors emerging for the first time).

Inspirations

The Road, Station Eleven, The Last of Us, Mad Max: Fury Road, Horizon Zero Dawn, Nausicaä

Sample Troubles
