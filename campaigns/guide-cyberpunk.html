⬡ Neon Abyss

Corporate Dystopia in Meridian

Meridian is a vertical city where altitude equals wealth. Corporations own the infrastructure, the law, and increasingly the bodies of their employees through augmentation contracts. The street economy runs on fixers, black clinics, and data brokerage. The net is throttled by district. The augments have kill switches.

Tone & Atmosphere

Noir-inflected techno-anxiety. The technology is personal - in your body, owned by someone else, knowing things about you that you don't. The corporations are profitable in a way that makes evil efficient.

For Players

You are runners, fixers, and ghosts in the machine. Your augments are your résumé and your leash. Every job pays the debt a little - and puts you deeper into someone else's ledger.

Key Factions

Megacorporations (OmniSec and others), street gangs (territorial), fixer networks (grey-market brokers), underground resistance, black clinics (outside the contract system), media operatives (the story is the weapon).

Inspirations

Neuromancer, Blade Runner, Cyberpunk Edgerunners, Ghost in the Shell, Akira, Mr. Robot
