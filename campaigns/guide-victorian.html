⊕ The Gaslight Chronicles

Gothic Cosmic Horror in Gaslit London

The Enlightenment promised reason would banish the darkness. It lied. In the gaslit streets, the veil between the rational and the incomprehensible is very thin. The Obsidian Lodge contains what it can. Scotland Yard investigates what it understands. And beneath everything — in the fog, in the mirrors — something ancient is paying attention.

Tone & Atmosphere

Investigative horror with a social dimension. Victorian society's rigid class structure is both shield and prison. The horror is not that the monsters exist — it's that they always have, and the rational world has been a polite agreement not to notice.

For Players

You are investigators, scholars, doctors, and outcasts drawn into cases that begin as mundane mysteries and end as confrontations with the incomprehensible. Your accent marks you. Your class follows you. The entities do not care about any of it.

Key Factions

The Obsidian Lodge (occult secret society), Scotland Yard (overwhelmed), rival lodges (competing rituals), the spiritualist movement (frauds and the genuine, indistinguishable), the entities themselves (they have rules — the rules are not in English).

Inspirations

Penny Dreadful, From Hell, The Prestige, Lovecraft, Bram Stoker, Arthur Machen, M.R. James
