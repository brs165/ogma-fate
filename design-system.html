Ogma Design Language

Field Dispatch

Physical, warm, tactile. Designed for game masters under pressure — at the table, in low light, on mobile. Cards that feel like something you could touch.

v2026.03.366 · WCAG 2.2 AA · Futura (Jost)
01

Principles

01
Physical Weight
Cards have shadow offset. Buttons press down when clicked. Everything feels like it exists in physical space — paper, ink, rubber stamps.
02
Warm, Not Cold
Dark mode is warm ink-black (#0E0C09), not neutral black. Light mode is cream (#F5F0E8), not white. Every surface feels like aged paper.
03
Legibility First
Every text element ≥ 11px. Futura (Jost proxy) throughout — geometric, clean, readable at arm's length at a lit table. One unified font family: display, body, labels, and UI chrome.
04
Deliberate Motion
Animations have weight. Stamp entries bounce slightly. Stress boxes scale up then settle. Clock ticks compress vertically. prefers-reduced-motion disables all of it instantly.
05
Ink Over Chrome
No glass morphism. No blur. Borders are solid. Shadows are offset squares, not soft clouds. The aesthetic is a zine printed on a good press, not an OS widget.
06
Complete Information
Cards expand to fit all content. No scroll. No truncation. No "see more." A GM reading a card mid-session gets everything in one look.

Components

Buttons — Stamp Press
All buttons use offset shadow that collapses on press. Hover lifts +2px. Active presses down 2px right + 2px down. prefers-reduced-motion disables the transform entirely.
